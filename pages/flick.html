Flick

Spring 2015

  • UX/UI
  • |
  • Visual Design
high fidelity mockups

Flick is a web application that allows that allows users to track and rate movies they watch, save movies that they want to watch in the future, and get customized recommendations based on their ratings. This idea was developed for the Interaction Design class at Northeastern University. I went through an extensive design process with sketching, wireframing, user testing, prototyping, and visual design.
