high fidelity mockups

At Intrepid Pursuits, I worked on an internal capstone project as a mobile app designer. With one other designer, we designed an app that allows for the administration of contests. Users can create a contest with specific scoring parameters, then invite contestants who can submit entries and judges who score the entries. Working from an initial set of user stories, we designed wireframes and high fidelity mockups to be passed on to developers. Throughout the entire process we received and made changes based on feedback from an internal client.

Wireframes

With Judgy, the intent was to create an experience that felt natural for all three user types - contest admins, contestants, and judges. We created patterns and components to be used across the three main user flows.

Look & Feel

When applying look and feel to the wireframes we presented 3 directions to the client and eventually decided upon the direction that was most restrained. By using a fairly minimal visual style, the content becomes the most important part of the app. This helps ensure the design will be sustainable across all types of contests and content.
