I recently graduated from Northeastern University with a B.S. in Computer Science & Interactive Media. Throughout my time at Northeastern I gained a deep interest not only in visual design, but the design process and how it can be leveraged to solve complex problems. Within Scout, Northeastern's student-run design studio, I founded a social innovation initiative that uses design thinking to tackle social issues in Boston. While at Northeastern I also had the opportunity to complete co-ops at Charles River Development and Placester.

Moving forward, I hope to use my breadth of knowledge in design thinking, visual design, user experience design, and technology to tackle social issues that I am passionate about (think social and economic inequality, the achievement gap, and global development). I'm currently interning at SMAKK Studios is New York City. I'm always trying to learn as much as possible from the people around me and seeking new perspective.

I also do some other things, like take photos and play music.
