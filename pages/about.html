I am currently in my last year of pursuing a combined degree in Computer Science & Interactive Media at Northeastern University in Boston. Throughout my time here at Northeastern I have gained a deep interest not only in visual design, but the design process and how it can be leveraged to solve big, complicated problems. After earning my degree, I hope to use my knowledge of design process, visual design, and technology to tackle social issues that I am passionate about (think social and economic inequality, the achievement gap, health and healthcare). Until then, I am just trying to learn as much as possible from the people around me.

I also do some other things, like take photos, play music, listen to podcasts and work at Scout, Northeastern's student run design studio. I helped found and am currently leading Scout Labs, Scout's social innovation initiative.
