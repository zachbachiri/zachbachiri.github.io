I am currently pursuing a combined degree in Computer Science & Interactive Media at Northeastern University in Boston. Throughout my time here at Northeastern I have gained a deep interest not only in visual design, but the design process and how it can be leveraged to solve problems. After earning my degree, I hope to use my knowledge of design process, visual design, and technology to solve social problems that I am passionate about.Until then, I am trying to learn as much as possible from the people around me.

I also do some other things, like take photos, play music, and work at Scout, Northeastern's student run design studio. I helped found and am currently leading Scout Labs, Scout's social innovation lab.
