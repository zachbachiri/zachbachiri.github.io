Spot is a mobile application that allows co-located users to create a collective music playlist for their party. Users can all dynamically add songs to a playlist and vote for their favorite songs. This project was completed for the Human-Computer Interaction course at Northeastern University with two other students. We created personas, storyboards, and wireframes; ran usability tests with paper prototypes; created high fidelity mockups; and prototyped the appliction with HTML and CSS. I contributed heavily to all aspects of the design process and was almost solely responsible for visual design.
