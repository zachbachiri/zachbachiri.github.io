Designer / Developer

I am currently pursuing a combined degree in Computer Science & Interaction Design at Northeastern University in Boston. Throughout my time here at Northeastern I have gained a deep interest only in visual design, but the design process and how it can be leveraged to solve problems. Ultimately, all I want to do is solve problems. After graduation I hope to find a problem that I am passionate about and use my design and development skills to solve it. Until then, I am trying to learn as much as possible from the people around me. I also do some other things, like take photos, play music, and work at Scout, Northeastern's student run design studio. I am currently in the process of helping launch and lead Scout Labs, Scout's social innovation lab.
